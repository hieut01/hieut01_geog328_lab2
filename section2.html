Section 2 — Key Innovations in Analytics

Key Innovations in Analytics List

  • Shot Charts – Maps that show where players shoot from and how often they make those shots.
  • Player Tracking Cameras – Systems like Second Spectrum that record every player’s movement during a game.
  • Data Websites – Sites like Basketball Reference make it easy for anyone to look up advanced stats.

These new tools changed how teams play. They started focusing more on three-pointers and layups because they’re worth more or easier to make, instead of long mid-range shots.
